
Makes the result of Futures#chain() cancellable.  It will now be
successfully canceled in these cases:
1. A thread calls cancel(boolean) directly on it and the Future is
currently running.
2. The underlying input Future is cancelled.
3. The Future result of the Function is cancelled.
