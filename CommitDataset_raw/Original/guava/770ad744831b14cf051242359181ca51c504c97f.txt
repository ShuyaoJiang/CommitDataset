Add a "constrainToRange" method to constrain a value to a closed interval.

Equivalent to Boost's clamp function [1] and numpy.clip [2].

Math.min(Math.max(x, min), max) is much more difficult to read and error-prone.

[1] http://www.boost.org/doc/libs/1_53_0/boost/algorithm/clamp.hpp

[2] https://docs.scipy.org/doc/numpy/reference/generated/numpy.clip.html
