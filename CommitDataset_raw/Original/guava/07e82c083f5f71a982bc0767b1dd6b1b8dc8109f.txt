Fix various collections (Sets.union(), Sets.intersection(), Multisets.sum(), etc.) that might overflow when asked for size(). With very large collections, these could potentially return negative (!) results. According to the spec, the result should be Integer.MAX_VALUE:

https://docs.oracle.com/javase/7/docs/api/java/util/Collection.html#size()
