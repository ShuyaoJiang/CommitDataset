Deprecate HttpUtil.getCharsetAsString(...) and introduce HttpUtil.getCharsetAsSequence(...).

Motivation:

The method HttpUtil.getCharsetAsString(...) is missleading as its return type is CharSequence and not String.

Modifications:

quence(...).
